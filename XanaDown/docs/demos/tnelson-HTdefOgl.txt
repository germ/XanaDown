=== 16.08.10

Let me introduce the word "hypertext" to mean a body of written or pictorial material interconnected in such a complex way that it could not conveniently be presented or represented on paper.

-- from Theodor H, Nelson, "A File Structure for the Complex, the Changing and the Indeterminate," Association for Computing Machinery: Proceedings of the 20th National Conference, 84-100. Ed. Lewis Winner, 1965.
