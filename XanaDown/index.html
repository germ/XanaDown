Wormwood Xanadoer

The Wormwood Xanadoer (and associated systems) are a fork of xanaviewer3 by Ted Nelson and his team at Cambridge. Learn more about Wormwood & Xanadu.

If you have the URL of a xanadoc Edit Decision List (EDL), enter it here to compile the document. You'll be taken to a viewer page with a permanent URL for your document.

Alternatively, you may edit and compile your own EDL here. See this document for more information on how to write a xanadoc EDL.

Furthermore, Wormwood supports Cambridge's dynamic xanadoc editor, which may be found here.
